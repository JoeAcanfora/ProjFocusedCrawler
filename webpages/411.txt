Publisher:

Publication Date:
30 October 2014
Tags:
Burkina Faso, Governance, Legal and Judicial Affairs



Tens of thousands of protesters gathered in the Burkina Faso capital Ouagadougou on October 28 in opposition to plans by President Blaise Compaore to extend his term of office. A rally in Place de la Nation in the heart of the capital was followed by a march through the streets, with police reportedly firing tear gas. In Bobo Dioulasso, protesters reportedly tore down a statue of Compaoré.
