Update: Suspect accused of exposing himself at local store turns himself in, police say

Posted 2:29 pm, September 2, 2014, by Ashton Edwards, Updated at 08:17pm, September 2, 2014 

FacebookTwitterPinterestLinkedInGoogleEmail 


– West Jordan Police
WEST JORDAN, Utah — A man accused of exposing himself and other lewd acts at a local store has turned himself in, according West Jordan Police tweets Tuesday evening.
Police said the suspect allegedly followed a female employee through the Asian City Market for about half an hour.
Officials said the man exposed himself multiple times and committed other lewd acts.
Authorities said the man is a regular customer and may live in the area.
The investigation into the incident is ongoing. Police say the suspect will be identified once all charges are filed.
