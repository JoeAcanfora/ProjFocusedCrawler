 DAILY NEWS   New York   News   Politics  Sports   Entertainment   Opinion   Living   Autos        Search          U.S     World     Crime     The Week     News Pics     Blogs             Metallica aids search for Virginia Tech student Morgan Dana Harrington missing from concert   BY   Eitan Gavish   DAILY NEWS WRITER Monday, October 26, 2009, 12:47 PM  A A A           Virginia State Police/AP Morgan Dana Harrington remains missing after attending a Metallica show on Oct. 17. The band has posted information about the disappearance on their website.    The search for a missing Virginia tech student who went missing during a Metallica concert has turned up no leads, despite a major search effort by police and aid from the band.Morgan Dana Harrington, 20, was last seen outside an Oct. 17 Metallica concert in Charlottesville, Va. She was attending the show at the University of Virginia’s arena with a group of friends, but had gone outside and was denied reentry under arena policy.  State Police spokeswoman Corinne Geller told The Associated Press that troopers and university police spent eight hours on foot in Charlottesville Saturday night, passing out flyers with pictures of Harrington.Harrington’s photograph was also displayed on the “Whoo-tron” during a University of Virginia football game on Saturday night. Geller told The Associated Press that no significant new evidence came to light during the weekend’s efforts. Metallica has offered an additional $50,000 to the Harrington family’s $100,000 reward for the safe return of their daughter, or information leading to an arrest and conviction of those responsible for her disappearance. The reward is posted on the search campaign site Findmorgan.com.On Oct. 20, Metallica posted information about the disappearance on its Web site, offering police contacts and a description and photos of Harrington. “We are deeply concerned about the disappearance,” the band states on the site. “Our thoughts are with Morgan and her family for her safe return.”     Related News  Virginia Tech student Morgan Dana Harrington, 20, vanishes from Metallica concert       'Missing' girl's dad: 'I am totally baffled'       Sheriff says body in landfill appears to be 7-year-old Somer Thompson       Somer Thompson disappearance: Investigators baffled by missing Florida girl
