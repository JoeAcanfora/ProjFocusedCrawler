Photo: Florida State, Nike Unveil New Seminoles-Themed Training Shoe
Matt Lombardi

Florida State fans who need to own every piece of official team-sponsored apparel will have a new item on their wish lists this winter. Thursday, the school, along with Nike, released a brand new Field General Training Shoe that features a black, garnet and gold color scheme and the Seminoles logo. Check it out:

The #Noles edition of the Nike Field General Training Shoe is now available http://t.co/7yC2yM5ZGg pic.twitter.com/AWIL5G4LCt
— Seminoles.com (@Seminoles_com) October 16, 2014
Here’s a bit more about the new kicks, via Seminoles.com:

They feature dynamic Flywire technology for lightweight lockdown, Nike Zoom units for the ultimate cushioning and a tough outsole that provides control.

They’ll run you $129.95 – enjoy, FSU fans.
