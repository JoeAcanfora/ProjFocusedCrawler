 	An FSU student who was in the school's main library said he was just feet away from a gunman who opened fire early Thursday morning.  	   	That student, Jason Derfuss, said in a Facebook post that the shooter fired at him. He said books in his backpack saved him from the bullet.  	   	Derfuss described his encounter and posted photos of the books that absorbed the bullet.  	   SEE THE PHOTOS HERE: http://bit.ly/1qzSSLx   	   	"He was about 5 feet from me, but he hit my books. Books one minute earlier I had checked out of the library, books that should not have stopped the bullet. But they did," Derfuss wrote.  	   	The full post is here:   		     Post  by Jason Derfuss .    	   Three students were injured in the shooting  before the gunman was shot dead by police.
