Photos: Jenn Sterger Is Back At Doak Campbell For FSU vs. Florida
Matt Lombardi

Back in 2005, long before Katherine Webb was even thinking about a modeling career, ABC announcer Brent Musburger made another female fan in the stands – Jenn Sterger – a household name. Her career has been a bit all over the place since, but it’s clear that Sterger still has some serious love for her Seminoles. Saturday, she somehow found her way back to the front row at Doak Campbell Stadium for another all-important in-state matchup. Check it out:
