Vine: Top RB Recruit Jacques Patrick Chooses Florida State Over Alabama, Florida, Texas A&M, Ohio State
Dan Lyons

Florida State has spent the better part of two years dominating on the field, and wins haven’t been too hard to come by on the recruiting trail for Jimbo Fisher’s program either. Today, FSU received a commitment from one of the nation’s highest rated running backs, Orlando native Jacques Patrick.

Jacques Patrick commits to FSU https://t.co/qYk4HHtlfN
— Josh Newberg (@joshnewberg247) October 27, 2014


Jacques Patrick chooses FSU pic.twitter.com/DmOWpzHpSD
— Charles King (@varsityfootball) October 27, 2014

Patrick chose FSU over numerous other powerhouse programs, including finalists Alabama, Florida, Ohio State, and Texas A&M. Patrick is a four star prospect according to Scout, 247Sports, and ESPN, and received five stars from Rivals.
