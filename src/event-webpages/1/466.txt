Florida State vs. Miami (FL) Will Kick Off At 8 P.M. EST On ABC
Bill DiFilippo

Saturday, Nov. 15 will feature what is annually one of the biggest college football games in the state of Florida, as the No. 2 Florida State Seminoles will travel to Sun Life Stadium to take on the Miami Hurricanes. The two schools share a rich rivalry, and while the Hurricanes lead the all-time series, 31-27, the Seminoles have won the last four meetings and seven of the last nine.
Today, it was announced that the game will be a primetime showdown. The two teams will square off at 8:00 P.M. EST on ABC.

FSU at Miami, 8pm, ABC
— Tim Reynolds (@ByTimReynolds) November 3, 2014

The matchup is still two weeks away, but we’re already looking forward to watching two of college football’s biggest programs square off in primetime. As for this week, Miami has a bye, while Florida State takes on Virginia.
