Typhoon Hagupit – in pictures









Preparations are made to evacuate families who live in the path of typhoon Hagupit as the storm makes its way towards the Philippines. The typhoon knocked out the power in entire coastal provinces, mowed down trees and sent more than 650,000 people into shelters before it weakened











Saturday 6 December 2014 19.25 EST
