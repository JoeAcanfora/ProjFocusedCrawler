Police search for missing Sugar Creek man

Posted 11:22 pm, September 23, 2014, by FOX 4 Newsroom 

FacebookTwitterPinterestGoogleLinkedInEmail 





SUGAR CREEK, Mo. — The Sugar Creek, Missouri Police Department is asking for help finding a missing man.
Christopher Anderson, 29, was last seen driving a blue Volkswagen Jetta.
He’s described as a white male, with brown eyes and brown hair. He stands about six feet tall and weighs about 180 pounds.
If you’ve seen him, call the TIPS Hotline at 816-474-TIPS.
