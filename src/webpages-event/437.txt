Clay County Sheriff: Missing man has been found

Posted 11:34 am, September 5, 2014, by Kasey Babbitt, Updated at 05:13pm, September 6, 2014 

FacebookTwitterPinterestGoogleLinkedInEmail 


KANSAS CITY, Mo. — The Clay County Sheriff’s office  said a man who has been missing since Thursday morning has been found.
Shortly after 4:35 p.m. on Saturday the sheriff’s office tweeted that “Joshua Jackson has been located. Family says he is safe.”
Jackson, 31, of Kansas City North, had last been seen near Barry Rd and 169 Highway at his work place at approximately 6 a.m. on Thursday. His vehicle was located in Kansas City North but police said at that time there was no sign of him.
Jackson suffers from Crohn’s disease and was in need of medication.
