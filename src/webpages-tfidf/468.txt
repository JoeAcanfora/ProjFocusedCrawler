Missing woman found, is OK police say

Posted 6:58 pm, July 15, 2014, by FOX 13 News, Updated at 07:30pm, July 15, 2014 

FacebookTwitterPinterestLinkedInGoogleEmail 


MILLCREEK, Utah — Unified Police has located a missing an endangered woman Tuesday evening.
Mon Rai, 62, of Nepal, was found in the area of 3400 South and 2700 East. Police say she is OK.
Rai is visiting family in Utah. According to police she went for a walk at 8 a.m. and didn’t return. Family reported her missing at 2:22 p.m.
 
Courtesy of Unified Police Police
