Timeline: The search for Hannah Graham
University of Virginia student Hannah Graham, 18, who is from Fairfax County, spent the night of Friday, Sept. 12, socializing and drinking with friends in Charlottesville, but hasn't been seen since the early morning hours of Sept. 13. A massive search is underway to find the missing student.
