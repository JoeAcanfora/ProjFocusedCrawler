Missing boy in SLC has been found

Posted 10:11 pm, July 15, 2014, by Tiffany DeMasters, Updated at 10:28pm, July 15, 2014 

FacebookTwitterPinterestLinkedInGoogleEmail 



 Courtesy of Salt Lake City Police 
SALT LAKE CITY — Salt Lake City Police stopped its search for a 6-year-old boy when he returned home on his bike Tuesday evening.
At approximately 9 p.m. police received a call that Elijah Hernandez was last seen leaving his home on a bike in the area of 1800 West and 1380 North.
The boy has since returned and is safe, police say.
